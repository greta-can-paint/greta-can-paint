About the Artist

Hi, I’m Greta Fanta, the artist behind Greta Can Paint. I am a recent graduate from UCLA and am currently living in San Diego. I adore crafting in all forms; while painting is my go-to, I also enjoy working with clay, wood, ribbon, fabric, and digital designing. I tend to lean more towards bold colors within my work and like to create a feeling of warmth and nostalgia.
